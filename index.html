Jay Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Stuffed Mushrooms

    mushroom caps filled with a savory mixture, often
    including breadcrumbs, garlic, cheese, and herbs.

  • Deviled eggs

    Hard-boiled eggs halved and filled with a creamy mixture made from
    the yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, and various seasonings. Garnished with
    paprika, chives, or bacon bits for an extra touch of flavor.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection,
    served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Jolloff rice with Grilled Chicken

    Aromatic and flavorful rice cooked in a savory tomato and pepper
    sauce, served with tender, spice-marinated grilled chicken. Garnished
    with fresh herbs and a side of fried plantains for a complete West
    African experience.

  • Moroccan Lamb Tagine

    Slow-cooked lamb simmered with vegetables, dried apricots, and a
    fragrant mix of Moroccan spices. Served in a traditional tagine pot
    with a side of fluffy couscous and warm, freshly baked flatbread.
    A hearty and flavorful dish that captures the essence of Moroccan
    cuisine.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmellows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Malva Pudding

    A warm, spongy South African dessert soaked in a rich, buttery caramel
    sauce. Served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of fresh
    cream.

  • Mango and coconut rice pudding

    A luscious blend of creamy coconut rice pudding topped with fresh,
    juicy mango slices. A tropical delight which is both refreshing and
    satisfying.
